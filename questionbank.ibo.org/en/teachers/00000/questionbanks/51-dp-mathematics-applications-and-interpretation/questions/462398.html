The following diagram shows the graph of  f .

There is a maximum point at A. The minimum value of f is −13 .

The distance, d centimetres, of the centre of the ball from O at time t seconds, is given by

d ( t ) = f ( t ) + 17 , 0 ⩽ t ⩽ 5.
